Profile

Vasu Gupta & Company

We are a team of dedicated professionals practicing from Lucknow with a core focus on audit, taxation, and forensic accounting. Founded on the principles of technical excellence and ethical practice, we deliver reliable counsel across every engagement.

CA Vasu Gupta leads the firm with extensive experience in navigating complex financial landscapes. In addition to core auditing expertise, he has successfully completed the Post Qualification Course in Information Systems Audit (ISA) from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), enabling the firm to bridge the gap between financial compliance and digital security.

As active members of the ICAI, we uphold the highest professional standards, serving a diverse clientele ranging from local retailers to large-scale charitable organizations.
